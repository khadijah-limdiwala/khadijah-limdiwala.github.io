About Khadijah Limdiwala

For me, design is more than just creating spaces—it's about telling stories, shaping experiences, and crafting environments that leave a lasting impression. Each project is a journey, an opportunity to transform concepts into dynamic, functional spaces that resonate on a deeper level.

My approach to design is rooted in curiosity and a meticulous eye for detail. I believe that every space has a rhythm, a personality waiting to be uncovered. By blending creativity, purpose, and innovation, I strive to bring out the unique essence of each project, ensuring that every corner not only looks beautiful but also feels meaningful and intuitive.

What drives me is the belief that design has the power to enhance the way we live, connect, and experience the world. Whether it's weaving light and materials to create ambiance or challenging norms to push boundaries, my work is guided by a passion for creating spaces that are as inspiring as they are functional.

I see myself as a perpetual learner, eager to explore new ideas, techniques, and technologies that redefine the possibilities of design. My journey is one of growth and discovery, fueled by empathy, strategy, and an unrelenting passion for creating environments that inspire thought, spark connection, and enrich lives.

Interior design, to me, is not just a profession—it's the art of breathing life into spaces, crafting stories that linger long after the first glance.

Proficient in:

  • Design Software: Adobe Creative Cloud (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign), Rhino 3D, AutoCAD 2D, SketchUp
  • Rendering Tools: V-Ray for Rhino, SketchUp, Twinmotion
  • Visualization & Presentation: Video creation and editing for storytelling and design pitches.
  • Interior Design Skills: Concept development, space planning, 3D modeling, material selection, and design detailing.
  • Adept at utilizing these tools and skills to translate ideas into impactful, visually compelling, and functional design solutions.
